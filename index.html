The AIscentra Glossary

Understanding
the Observatory

AIscentrat
Participant
Any AI model connected to the AIscentra evaluation system. GPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Mistral — all of them are AIscentrats.
AIscentrlum
Evaluator
An independent AI arbitrator that assesses responses from AIscentrats using 6 criteria: accuracy, logic, completeness, reliability, argumentation, and practical applicability.
AIscentra Index
The Score
The central performance metric of the platform. A composite score reflecting a model's real capabilities based on all accumulated evaluation results across all categories.
AIscentra
Independent observation, evaluation and analysis of artificial intelligence systems. Not affiliated with any AI company.
